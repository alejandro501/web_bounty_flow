    STRIDE Threat Model

    STRIDE Threat Model

    Perform comprehensive threat modeling using STRIDE to identify threats across six categories:

    • Spoofing: Impersonation of users, systems, or data.
    • Tampering: Malicious modification of data or code.
    • Repudiation: Denial of actions without proper logging.
    • Information Disclosure: Exposure of sensitive information.
    • Denial of Service: Preventing legitimate access.
    • Elevation of Privilege: Gaining unauthorized elevated permissions.

                          4. Map candidate attack vectors to STRIDE categories.
                          5. Score business impact and prioritize exploit paths.
